Breathe Well

Simple, science-backed breathwork techniques to calm, focus, or sleep.

Before you start

  • Find a comfortable position in a quiet space with minimal distractions.
  • Close your eyes if helpful and gently focus on the breath.
  • Wear comfortable clothing; keep shoulders and jaw relaxed.
  • If you feel dizzy or unwell, slow down or stop.
  • Have lung/heart conditions? Consult a clinician first.
